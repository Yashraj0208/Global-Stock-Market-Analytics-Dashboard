Global Stock Market Analytics Dashboard


Project Overview -
This project is a three-page interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze global stock market performance, company profitability, and investment opportunities across multiple countries.

The dashboard enables users to explore financial metrics, compare company performance, and identify potential investment opportunities through interactive visualizations and dynamic filtering.


Business Objective
- To provide a comprehensive overview of global stock market performance.
- To evaluate company profitability using key financial metrics.
- To identify high-performing companies and attractive investment opportunities.
- To support data-driven financial analysis through interactive reporting.



Tools Used
- Power BI
- DAX
- Power Query
- Data Modeling
- Interactive Visualizations



Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

Page 1 – Global Stock Market Overview
- Total Market Capitalization
- Total Companies
- Total Countries

Page 2 – Profitability Analysis
- Total Earnings
- Total Revenue
- Average P/E Ratio

Page 3 – Investment Opportunity Finder
- Average Dividend Yield
- Average P/E Ratio
- High Dividend Yield Companies



Dashboard Features

Global Stock Market Overview
- Global market capitalization distribution (Map)
- Top 10 companies by market capitalization
- Country-wise market analysis

Profitability Analysis
- Top 10 companies by earnings
- Revenue vs Earnings scatter plot
- Companies reporting losses (Matrix)

Investment Opportunity Finder
- Top 10 companies by dividend yield
- Top 10 countries by average dividend yield
- High dividend yield company analysis



Interactive Features
- Country slicer available on all dashboard pages
- Dynamic KPI updates
- Interactive charts and visuals
- Cross-filtering between visuals
- Drill-down business insights


Key Insights
- Market capitalization varies significantly across countries and companies.
- A small number of companies contribute a large share of global market value.
- Revenue and earnings relationships help identify profitable businesses.
- Dividend yield analysis highlights potential investment opportunities.
- Country-level filtering enables comparative financial analysis.
